BRIDGE The future of communication
The Overview
The purpose of creating BRIDGE was to create an app that would streamline communication through miltiple language translations, as well as help all users better understand the customs and cultures of different countries..
The Problem
Current language apps are limited in their language choices, contain many errors, and are costly. This can cause one to communicate incorrectly. This, in turn can cause quite a bit of unecessary stress.
The Solution
A mobile application that is not only a seamless translator that operates in real-time via various chat applications, but will also support better understanding a country’s customs and culture so as not to stress over causing offense.
Empathize User Reaserch
The Hypothosis
If people across all walks of life better understand other cultures' customs, and can communicate with fewer mistakes, they will have far less stress.
The User Interviews & Surveys
Input from many individuals allowed us to better understand the frustrations regarding interactions with those of a different language.
The User Persona
The Personas were showcased to elaborate on and diversify where communication mishaps can happen, and who is affected.
Define The Problem
The Problem Statement
We believe that creating a mobile communication app that will also educate students, employees & travelers of important cultural customs, will help bridge the gap allowing more diverse, stress-free and meaningful communication.
