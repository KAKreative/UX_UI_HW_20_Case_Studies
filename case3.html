The Overview
The purpose of the SOS project was to create a simplistic, easy to navigate site for users to have access to all the information they may need when caring for a newly adopetd or fostered pet.
The Problem
The current website is not delivering an easy & enjoyable experience for users seeking information about adopting a pet. This is causing users to quickly leave the site.
The Solution
A website that is simplified and easier to navigate will invite users to enjoy exploring the website in it’s entirety. This will result in increased adoption and fostering of pets.
Empathize User Reaserch
The Hypothosis
If elements of the site are easier to read and navigate, it will increase a user's access to relevant information resulting in smoother adoption, foster, & donations.
The User Interviews & Surveys
Receiving valuable input from travelers allowed me to better understand the frustrations and expectations regarding traveling with pets.
The User Persona
Our Persona and Storyboard were created based on the team members' ideas, assumptions, and experiences with animal rescue.

The Problem Statement
We believe that by improving the Saving Orphan Souls Rescue site, customers will be successful in finding all of the information they need to confidentally adopt and foster a pet.
