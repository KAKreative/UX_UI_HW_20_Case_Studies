TOTO A Travel App
The Overview
The purpose of the TOTO project was to create a multi-functional all-in-one travel app centered around the four-legged family members. It was important that we kept the aesthetics and interface simple, yet enjoyable.
The Problem
Most apps including a variety of flight, accommodations, and sightseeing data, are over-complicated, and do not cater to the traveling “pet parent.”
The Solution
A mobile app that is an easy and simplified way to not only find the best rates when traveling with your four-legged family member, but to locate accommodations with special pet-specific features.
Empathize User Reaserch
The Hypothosis
If pet-parents can book travel with less hassel and stress, humans will travel more often due to fewer limitations.
The User Interviews & Surveys
Receiving valuable input from travelers allowed me to better understand the frustrations and expectations regarding traveling with pets.
